our services

We are a mobile app development, web development consultant and Axie scholarship company helping people around the globe

Software development

Our software factory offers solutions to small companies and individuals who need a website or a mobile application.

We create software that fully accommodates your unique business and industry capabilities, eliminating the time-consuming and complex customization required for software products available on the market.

3D Modeling

Do you need full 3D game modeling for your game, or just one area to materialize? We do it cheaply and with quality. We can work alone or with others. You can also tailor the design to the resources available and the strengths of your team.

We provide anyone with any model or animation they need, whether it’s for VRChat or another 3D emulator, or for a game they are developing.

 

Game coaching (Axie Infinity)

This Game coaching is designed to train and test ambitious scientists interested in discovering the financial freedom that Axie Infinity can offer. Covers the fundamentals of cryptography, the Axie Infinity white paper, a complete PvE/PvP guide, and more.

To help you assemble your new team or to take another look at your current team strategy, we can offer you free coaching if you are a member of the community or paid sessions if you require deeper analysis and play-testing.
